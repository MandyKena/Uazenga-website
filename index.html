The beauty of Namibia

Namibia is a beautiful country in the southwestern of Africa, it is famous for vast desert, dramatic landscapes and also a unique wildlife. Its name means "vast place". The regions among the oldest and highest in the world, they glow golden at sunrise. Etosha National Park hosts different types of wildlife like elephants, rhinos and lions gathering at waterholes. Namibia has an endless desert that meets the cold Atlantic ocean, the Skeleton Coast mixes foggy shores and shipwrecks. Rich cultures like Himba and San add heritage. Namibia is one of the most magical places on earth, with wide-open spaces and quite beauty.

Top Spots
